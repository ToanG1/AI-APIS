Here is a simple recipe for making cookies:

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:
- 2 1/4 cups (280g) all-purpose flour 
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup (230g) unsalted butter, softened to room temperature
- 3/4 cup (150g) granulated sugar
- 3/4 cup (165g) brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2 large eggs
- 2 cups (340g) semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 375¡F (190¡C). Line two baking sheets with parchment paper.

2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda and salt. 

3. In a large bowl, beat the butter and sugars until creamed, about 2 minutes. Beat in vanilla and eggs until combined. 

4. Gradually stir in the dry ingredients until just combined. Fold in the chocolate chips. 

5. Scoop rounded tablespoon sized balls of dough and place them 2 inches apart on the baking sheets. 

6. Bake for 8-10 minutes, until edges are lightly browned. 

7. Allow cookies to cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

Tips:
- Let the butter soften at room temperature for at least 30 mins before baking.
- Use an ice cream scoop to portion cookie dough evenly. 
- Store baked cookies in an airtight container at room temperature up to 1 week.
